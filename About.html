About Me

My name is Jeroen Nijkamp. I would describe myself as creative and open minded individual. I was born on the 19th of October in 1992 in the Netherlands, in a city called Den Bosch. By the time i was 12 I had lived in 4 countries and 3 different continents. I enjoy looking at everyday things, ideas, situations etc from a different perspective. I am highly motivated by innovation, and i constantly look to the world around me for inspiration. I am by trade and by hobby, a designer, specializing in visual design. Currently I am studying User Experience Design at THUAS in the Netherlands, and am currently living in Den Haag.
